About Blockopedia Bedrock

Blockopedia Bedrock is a comprehensive online resource dedicated to Minecraft: Bedrock Edition. From tutorials and game mechanics to command explanations and tips for add-on creation, our mission is to help players and creators master the Bedrock Edition platform.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced builder, this wiki provides simplified, accurate, and updated information designed specifically for the Bedrock community.

How to Use Add-ons

1. Download a Bedrock-compatible add-on (usually in .mcaddon or .mcpack format).
2. Open the file with Minecraft, and it will automatically import.
3. Go to "Settings" > "Storage" to confirm it's installed.
4. Create or edit a world, then enable the add-on under "Behavior Packs" or "Resource Packs".
5. Launch the world and enjoy the new features!

How to Use Texture Packs

1. Download the texture pack (.mcpack format recommended).
2. Open it with Minecraft and wait for the import confirmation.
3. Navigate to "Settings" > "Global Resources" and apply the texture pack.
4. Alternatively, apply it to a specific world under "Resource Packs".
5. Enjoy your customized Minecraft visuals!
